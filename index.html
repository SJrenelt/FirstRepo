Introduction

I started college after taking two gaps years out of high school to gain job experience before college. This wasn't plan A, I originally was accepted and really to attend FIT in South Korea for their fashion design program, but couldn't get my student visa before the semester started because of COVID. I then was accepted to Pratt and attended for part of a semester for pattern design and realized it was way too expensive.

I then worked in wardrobe for TV and film, and then went on to do contract positions in the fashion industry including working as a PR assistant for H&M and in fashion wholesale. I was extremely unhappy with the work I was doing in fashion wholesale and made a huge decision to go back to school. I took college classes in high school so I had enough credits to cover almost 4 semesters of transfer credits.

Now, I'm graduating next fall from City Tech with my bachelor's in COMD. After graduation I plan to work for a few years while also attending graduate school to get my masters in biology. My end goal is to be a scientific illustrator and have my technical and vector illustrations in textbooks, field guides, advertisements, and informational materials.
